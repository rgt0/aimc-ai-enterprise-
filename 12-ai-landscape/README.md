AI Landscape
12-ai-landscape

Purpose:
Provide an orientation map of the Deutsche Telekom
enterprise AI ecosystem.

This section answers:

- What AI systems exist?
- What are they used for?
- How do they connect?
- Where can they be accessed?
- Who owns them?

This section does NOT provide
operational instructions or user guides.
